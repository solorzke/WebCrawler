2008 has seen many sequels and prequels in video games. New intellectual properties include Army of Two, Dead Space, Left 4 Dead, LittleBigPlanet, Mirror's Edge, Race Driver: Grid and Spore. Notable events of 2008 in video gaming. See also history of video games. The release dates listed in this article are the games' original release dates.
